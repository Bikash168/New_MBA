Professional Life Memberships

Connecting our academic programs, students, and faculty with leading national management associations to drive leadership and institutional excellence.

Professional Life Memberships

The MBA Department is a life member of prestigious national and international professional bodies. These affiliations establish direct pathways for industry networking, institutional benchmarking, learning resource sharing, and professional certifications.

Association of Indian Management Schools (AIMS)

As one of the largest networking networks of business schools globally, AIMS provides our faculty and students with advanced management research tools, regional case-writing seminars, and access to national management education benchmark standards.

National Human Resource Development Network (NHRDN)

The premier national body committed to grooming human capital leaders in India. Our partnership offers direct interaction with corporate directors, local HR chapter workshops, and leadership development programs to keep our students aligned with global talent trends.

Indian Society for Training & Development (ISTD)

ISTD serves as the national forum for HR trainers and learning coordinators. Membership provides our faculty with advanced pedagogical training resources, and opens exclusive pathways for students to obtain diplomas in training and development.
