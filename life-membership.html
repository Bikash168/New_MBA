Professional Memberships

The MBA Department is a life member of the following prestigious professional bodies, establishing direct pathways for industry networking, institutional benchmarking, and professional qualifications.

Association of Indian Management Schools (AIMS)

AIMS is one of the largest networking organizations of business schools in the world. Life membership provides our students and faculty with resources for management research, regional case-writing seminars, and access to national management education benchmark standards.

National Human Resource Development Network (NHRDN)

NHRDN is the premier national body committed to grooming human capital leaders in India. Our partnership offers direct interaction with corporate directors, local HR chapter workshops, and leadership development programs to keep our students aligned with global talent trends.

Indian Society for Training & Development (ISTD)

ISTD is the national forum for HR trainers and learning coordinators. Membership provides our faculty with training materials and resources for advanced methodologies, and opens opportunities for students to obtain diplomas in training and development.
