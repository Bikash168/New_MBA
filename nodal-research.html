Research & Doctoral Programme

The MBA Department of Trident Academy of Technology has been officially recognized as a Nodal Center of Research (NCR) by Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT) in 2023. This recognition highlights our focus on facilitating quality doctoral guidance, scholarly research, and academic-industry synergy.

Doctoral Scholars Guidance

At present, eight research scholars are actively enrolled in the Ph.D. programme under the guidance of our experienced and qualified faculty members. The center provides a highly supportive academic environment, facilitating structured coursework, review boards, and scholarly mentorship.

Research Thrust Areas

  • Human Resource Management & Green HR
  • Marketing & Digital SME Transformations
  • Corporate Finance & Financial Risk Valuations
  • Operations Strategy & Logistics Management
  • Sustainable Business & Social Innovation

Academic & Industry Impact

The department actively encourages collaborative research, publications in Scopus and UGC-CARE listed journals, and paper presentations at national conferences. Through these initiatives, the center aims to contribute meaningful business insights for policymakers and the corporate community.
