Meet Our Faculty & Mentors

Experienced academicians and industry practitioners who combine research expertise with applied teaching to coach the next generation of leaders.

Core Faculty

Our core faculty bring academic rigor and industry experience across strategy, finance, marketing, and operations.

PM
Prof. P. K. Mohanty
Dean — School of Business (SOB)
Former Head of the Department of MBA, Utkal University and Director (CD) BPUT, Odisha. Heading a stellar management faculty of 16, including 10 PhD holders.
AS
Dr. A. N. Sharma
Professor — Strategy & Innovation
Dr. Sharma's research focuses on strategic renewal and digital transformation. He has published in top-tier journals and consults for several Fortune 500 firms.
LR
Prof. L. K. Rao
Associate Professor — Finance
Prof. Rao specialises in corporate finance, valuations, and capital markets. He teaches advanced corporate finance and leads the finance lab.

Visiting Faculty & Industry Mentors

A rotating roster of visiting scholars and leaders who enrich our programs with current industry best practices and case studies.

Dr. R. K. Nair
Visiting — Operations
Expert in supply chain analytics and lean operations with consultancy experience across manufacturing.
Ms. S. Gupta
Mentor — HR & Leadership
Leadership coach focusing on talent strategy, organisational design, and executive coaching.
