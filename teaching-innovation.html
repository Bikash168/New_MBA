Teaching Innovations & Pedagogy

The MBA Department of Trident Academy of Technology employs several active teaching strategies designed to bridge the gap between classroom concept maps and industry-ready execution.

📚

Case Study Methodology

Classroom theories are applied directly to actual corporate challenges. Using case studies from Harvard Business Publishing, Ivey, and leading national journals, scholars learn critical thinking and out-of-the-box business problem-solving.

🗺️

Fieldwork & Experiential

Students participate in summer internships and regular industrial visits. The program integrates live industry survey projects where students compile data on local business trends and market behaviors.

📊

Instrumented Feedback

Continuous performance monitoring through structured feedback loops, peer performance evaluations, and individual assignments. This ensures tailored developmental support for every scholar.

🏆

Co-curricular Action

Active participation in national budget analysis competitions, inter-B-school paper presentations, HR conclaves, and corporate conclave fests to foster team management and communication.
