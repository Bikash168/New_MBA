TSBA MBA Admissions 2026

Take the next step in your management career. Explore our AICTE-approved, industry-connected MBA specializations and enrollment procedures.

Next-Gen Offerings

AICTE Approved Specialist MBA Programs

Choose a dynamic pathway structured for modern venture creation or environmental sustainability.

01 🚀

MBA IEV (Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Venture Development)

A 2-year specialized program focusing on innovation methodologies, business startup workflows, and value creation. Ideal for developing leaders capable of launching and running dynamic business ventures or driving corporate change.

Career Prospects
  • New Venture Creation
  • Innovation Management
  • Managing Startups
  • Venture Capital (VC)
  • Angel Investing
  • Management Consulting
  • Corporate Innovation
  • Product Management
02 🌱

MBA in Environmental Management (EM)

A 2-year specialized program designed to integrate social development, environmental risk assessment, and resource management to preserve ecological balance while driving sustainable corporate growth.

Career Prospects
  • Environmental Consulting
  • Corporate Sustainability
  • Policy & Advocacy
  • Compliance Management
  • Sustainable Development
  • Renewable Energy
  • Resource Management
  • Eco-Education & Outreach

💼 Starting Salary Range: Rs. 5 Lakhs to Rs. 10 Lakhs p.a.

Sanctioned Intake Capacity

Trident has a sanctioned strength of 60 seats for admission in the 2026 academic batch. The management will admit all 60 candidates based on scores obtained in national/state entrance tests.

OJEE 2026 MAT CAT XAT CMAT ATMA

* TSBA is fully approved by AICTE and affiliated with Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT), Odisha.

Core Specialisations

Dual specialization opportunities are available across functional business streams:

  • HR Management
  • Finance Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Systems Management
  • International Business

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must satisfy one of the following academic paths:

Three-Year Bachelor's Degree: Passed or appearing in 2026 for a minimum three-year Bachelor's degree examination from any University of Odisha or recognized UGC/AICTE institute.
Engineering / Tech / Pharmacy: Passed or appearing in 2026 for a minimum four/five year Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Technology, or Pharmacy from any Odisha or recognized UGC/AICTE institute.

⚠️ Minimum Marks: 50% in aggregate (45% for SC/ST category) at the qualifying examination.

🕒 Age Limit: There is no age limit for taking admission into the MBA course.

Selection Procedure

Admission to the MBA (Full time 2 year) program is strictly based on performance and ranks in entrance tests.

Entrance Exam Ranks: Candidate selection follows counseling guidelines set by OJEE, BPUT, and the Govt. of Odisha, utilizing scores from:
  • OJEE 2026 (Odisha Joint Entrance Exam)
  • MAT (Management Aptitude Test)
  • CAT / XAT / CMAT / ATMA

Shortlisted candidates are required to register on the OJEE counseling portal or approach the TSBA admission desk with valid scorecards and certificates.

Financial Aid & Incentives

Merit Scholarships & Fee Waivers

TSBA offers lucrative scholarship schemes and fee waivers based on academic merit and performance in selection rounds.

1st Year Merit Scholarships

  • Rs. 50,000/- Scholarship: Scored above 70% marks in each of Class-X, Class-XII, and Graduation.
  • Rs. 30,000/- Scholarship: Scored between 60% and 70% in each of Class-X, Class-XII, and Graduation.
  • Rs. 25,000/- Scholarship: Average percentage across Class-X, Class-XII, and Graduation exceeds 60%.

GD & Interview Slot Booking

Candidates selected through the group discussion and interview process will qualify for an additional 50% Fee waiver in the first year.

How to participate: Send your softcopy biodata to scholar@tat.ac.in to register.
