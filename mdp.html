Management Development Programme (MDP)

Promoting sustainability, corporate competence, and visionary human resource leadership through advanced development programmes.

2-Day Management Development Programme (MDP)

GREEN HR FOR SUSTAINABLE GROWTH 🌱
📅 Date: 11th–12th July 2025
📍 Venue: School of Business, Trident Academy of Technology, Bhubaneswar

The Management Development Programme (MDP) was a successful convergence of HR professionals, academicians, and industry leaders committed to driving sustainability through innovative human resource practices.

The programme was organized by the School of Business, Trident Academy of Technology in collaboration with the National Institute of Personnel Management (NIPM), Utkal Chapter. The event sparked meaningful discussions and provided practical insights into how HR can champion sustainable development.

A total of 21 participants from 12 leading organizations attended the intensive training sessions.

Participating Organizations

PPL Tata Steel OHPC OPTCL Emami Paper AM/NS India NALCO Talcher Fertilizer SAIL – RSP OMC Rungta Sons Pvt. Ltd. IDVB Recycling Operations Pvt. Ltd.
