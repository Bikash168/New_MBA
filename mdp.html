2-Day Management Development Programme (MDP)

Green HR for Sustainable Growth 🌱

A successful convergence of HR professionals, academicians, and industry leaders committed to driving sustainability through innovative human resource practices. The programme was organized by the School of Business, Trident Academy of Technology in collaboration with the National Institute of Personnel Management (NIPM), Utkal Chapter.

📅
Programme Dates
11th – 12th July 2025
📍
Venue
School of Business, Trident Academy of Technology, Bhubaneswar
👥
Participation
21 professional participants from 12 leading national organizations

This intensive program sparked meaningful discussions and provided practical insights into how modern HR operations can champion sustainable development, waste reduction, green branding, and eco-friendly employee engagement.

Participants engaged in collaborative workshops, case study analyses, and expert-led panels, bridging academic theory with corporate sustainability standards.

Participating Organizations

PPL Tata Steel OHPC OPTCL Emami Paper AM/NS India NALCO Talcher Fertilizer SAIL – RSP OMC Rungta Sons Pvt. Ltd. IDVB Recycling Operations Pvt. Ltd.
