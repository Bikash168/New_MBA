Alumni Meet – "Reverence"

Trident Academy of Technology proudly hosts its annual alumni event, "Reverence". This special occasion serves as a focal point for graduates across batches to reconnect, share achievements, and relive their memorable academic journeys.

Key Objectives:

  • Fostering ongoing communication and trust between the institute and its alumni.
  • Strengthening the Industry-Institute Interface to promote placements and projects.
  • Enhancing educational excellence through joint student, faculty, and alumni collaboration.

Visits & Guest Interactions

The institute invites distinguished alumni from diverse industries and geographical hubs to share real-world perspectives. These engagements occur during induction meets, guest lectures, and transition modules to orient current students.

Engagement Goals:

  • Highlighting the achievements and expansion of our alumni network.
  • Facilitating continuous knowledge exchange and corporate readiness workshops.
  • Strengthening collaborative project guidance and mentoring.

Placement Assistance & Briefings

TSBA alumni play a crucial role in the placement process. By actively sharing job vacancies and career tracks in their respective firms, they keep the placement cell well-aligned with corporate opportunities.

Furthermore, our graduates conduct sectoral briefings, outline industry roles, and hold mock preparation rounds. Their guidance significantly boosts student employability, paving the way for smooth transitions into corporate roles.

  • Direct Vacancy Sharing
  • Industry Sector Briefings
  • Mock Interview Preparation
  • Corporate Referral Programs
