Academic Programs

Program Structure & Curriculum

Explore the rigorous syllabus, course structures, and credit requirements for the BBA and MBA programs at Trident School of Business Administration.

Undergraduate Program

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

The undergraduate curriculum follows the AICTE Model Syllabus (2024 Guidelines) offering a flexible duration of 3 to 4 years (6 or 8 Semesters) with multiple entry and exit points. A balanced mix of Core, Ability Enhancement, Skill Enhancement, and Discipline Specific Elective courses prepares students for modern corporate challenges.

Postgraduate Program

MBA - First Year (Core Foundation)

The first year of the MBA programme at TSBA focuses on building a solid managerial foundation. Formulated for the admission batch of 2024-25, the curriculum covers core business functions, quantitative analytics, communication, and basic artificial intelligence skills.

Specialized Electives

MBA - Second Year (Specializations)

The Second Year (Semester III) is primarily dedicated to elective specializations. Students must choose any **two** specializations from the specified groups. In addition, students complete the Summer Internship Project sessional practical.
