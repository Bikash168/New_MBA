Premier Institution & Campus Partner

Strengths of Our Management Program

Nurturing Future Business Leaders

Our MBA Department is accredited by the Govt of India's National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for its overall quality of teaching-learning.

We emphasize a robust industry-focused curriculum, hands-on learning experiences, and engaging club activities to groom future business leaders. Our program offers specializations in Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, Systems, International Business, and Sustainability Management.
