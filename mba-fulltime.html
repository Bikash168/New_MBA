    Transformative, immersive two-year management program.

    Our full-time MBA blends rigorous academics, industry mentorship, and hands-on projects to prepare graduates for leadership across sectors.

    Students gain analytical skills, strategic thinking, and real-world experience through consulting projects, internships, and cross-functional labs.

    Program Snapshot

    Duration: 2 years · Intake: July · Mode: Full-Time · Campus: Bhubaneswar

    Core courses: Accounting, Finance, Marketing, Strategy, Operations, Organizational Behaviour.
    Electives & Labs: Data Analytics, Digital Marketing, Corporate Finance Lab, Consulting Practicum.
    Capstone & Internship: Industry project and 8-12 week summer internship.

    Learning Outcomes

    Graduates will be ready to lead strategy, manage teams, and deliver measurable business impact from day one.

    Analytical Rigor

    Build quantitative and qualitative analysis skills to solve complex business problems.

    Industry Immersion

    Live projects and internships with corporate partners provide direct exposure to workplace challenges.

    Leadership & Ethics

    Focused coaching on leadership, communication, and responsible decision-making.
