Program Overview

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Established in 2008, the Trident School of Business offers a two-year full-time MBA programme approved by AICTE & Government of Odisha, and affiliated to BPUT. As a premier accredited B-school, we deliver outcome-based management education focused on grooming future business leaders.

Vision & Mission Curriculum Details
60 Seats Approved Intake
NBA Accredited
2-Year Full-Time Mode

Program Snapshot

Outcome-based education standards affiliated with BPUT and recognized by AICTE.

Intake Capacity: Approved intake of 60 students per batch.
Duration & Affiliation: 2-year full-time study, affiliated with Biju Patnaik University of Technology.
Key Specializations: Curriculums covering core functional areas: Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, and Information Management.

VISION

To be a premier management institute shaping the next generation of impactful business leaders capable of transforming industries for sustainable growth.

🎯

MISSION

  • Empower students to create value for business and society with integrity.
  • Cultivate management professionals to navigate complexity and drive innovation in a rapidly evolving global economy.
  • Prepare future leaders who embrace diversity and inspire change for a sustainable future.
Academic Architecture

Rigorous & Pedagogical Approach

Delivering outcome-based education practices in management studies that align academic rigor with industry standards.

Centrally Air-Conditioned Campus

The academic environment is strengthened through modern infrastructure, including a centrally air-conditioned academic block with spacious lecture theatres.

Each theatre is equipped with advanced audio-visual teaching aids and intranet facilities for seamless access to online learning resources and student portals.

Innovative Pedagogies

To enhance the effectiveness of management education, the MBA programme integrates fieldwork, case study analysis, live projects, internships, and instrumented feedback.

Our experienced faculty members actively emphasize both conceptual clarity and practical application to shape competent and ethical management professionals.

Professional Development

Students actively participate in various co-curricular activities such as Inter B-School competitions, paper presentations, budget analysis contests, and field studies.

These exercises develop key leadership qualities, analytical thinking, and managerial competencies beyond the prescribed university curriculum.

Experiential Learning Clubs

The department encourages holistic development through active engagement in student-driven clubs including the Fintech Club, Strategy Club, Business Analytics Club, Communication Club, and Cultural Club.

These clubs provide platforms for experiential learning, teamwork, creativity, and leadership enhancement.

Admissions Procedure

Apply online for the 2-year full-time MBA program. Merit scholarships are available for deserving candidates.

How to Apply:

  1. Complete the online application form on our homepage.
  2. Upload academic transcripts, scorecard (MAT/CAT/XAT/OJEE), and certificates.
  3. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a personal interview and group discussion.
