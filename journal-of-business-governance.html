About the Journal

The Journal of Business Governance Insights (JBGI) is the flagship annual, peer-reviewed academic publication of the Department of Business Administration at the Trident School of Business Administration, Bhubaneswar.

Serving as a high-quality interdisciplinary platform, the journal publishes original research studies, empirical papers, corporate case analyses, and conceptual models. Our core mission is to bridge business theory with corporate governance realities, providing managers, academicians, and policy advocates with actionable insights.

The journal enforces a rigorous double-blind peer-review workflow under our dedicated editorial board. We seek to foster thought leadership and provide sustainable answers to corporate, financial, and developmental complexities.

Thematic Focus Areas

1

Corporate Governance & Ethics

Research on board oversight models, transparency indexes, executive rewards, shareholder activation, and business ethics.

2

ESG & Sustainability Audits

Analyses of corporate environmental stewardship, social accountability standards, and sustainable business models.

3

Financial Regulation & Analytics

Studies investigating credit rating workflows, macroeconomic policies, banking governance, and fiscal stability.

4

Digital Governance & Policy

Assessing AI governance guidelines, e-governance standards in services, and technology policy for small-scale enterprises.
