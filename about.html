Our Heritage

Genesis & History

Established in 2005, Trident School of Business Administration (TSBA) was founded with a vision to provide world-class management education in Odisha. Over the past two decades, TSBA has grown from a pioneering local institute into one of the state's premier business schools, continually evolving its pedagogy and infrastructure to meet global corporate demands.

2005

Trident School of Business Administration is founded under the aegis of the Trident Group of Institutions, with a goal of offering premium value-centric business training.

2013

Expanded corporate partnerships, introduced executive seminars, and integrated structured internships into the core MBA and BBA pedagogical curriculums.

2019

Accredited by the Government of India's prestigious National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for overall teaching excellence and program infrastructure.

Today

A leading, dynamic business school in Odisha featuring state-of-the-art Finance, Analytics, and Communication laboratories with strong Fortune 500 placements.

Our Parent Group

About Trident Group of Institutions

Trident Group of Institutions, established in 2005 in Bhubaneswar, is one of the leading educational conglomerates in Odisha. Offering courses in Engineering (TAT), Management (TSBA), Computer Applications (TCA), and Biotechnology, the group is renowned for its academic rigor, research initiatives, and massive recruitment networks.

With a sprawling green campus, state-of-the-art labs, and over 15,000 alumni worldwide, the Trident Group continues to shape student success and create future-ready tech and management leaders.
