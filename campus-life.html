Life at Trident Campus

Explore academic resources, centrally air-conditioned spaces, sports platforms, and dynamic student-led conclaves.

Academic Infrastructure

Trident features centrally air-conditioned academic blocks and tiered lecture theatres designed for modern pedagogy. Interactive displays, high-speed intranet terminals, and visual aids keep classrooms fully connected for outcome-based studies.

  • Centrally Air-Conditioned Blocks
  • Spacious Tiered Lecture Theatres
  • Digitized Audio-Visual Teaching Aids
  • Intranet Access & Portal Terminals
📖

TSBA Knowledge Hub (Library)

The library acts as a core learning repository, housing thousands of physical textbooks, business cases, and references. Students have digital portal subscriptions to leading international management journals, databases, and e-books.

  • Extensive Reference Catalog
  • Subscribed Global Journals
  • Electronic Databases Portals
  • Silent Collaborative Study Zone

Clubs & Recreation

A vibrant student-driven environment supported by 10 specialized business clubs. These clubs promote peer-learning and leadership skills, backed by sports fields and recreational activity spaces across the campus.

  • Fintech, BFSI, & Strategy

  • Communication & Arts Hubs
  • Outdoor & Indoor Courts

Annual Conclaves & Fests

Students drive major events annually, including National Marketing, HR, and Finance Conclaves. The fests provide platform for creative expressions, budget presentations, inter-institutional games, and cultural performances.

  • National Specialization Conclaves
  • Inter B-School Contests
  • Creative Arts Festivals
  • Corporate Guest Seminars

Industrial Visits

The MBA Department of Trident Academy of Technology regularly organises industrial visits to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world business practices. These visits provide students with valuable exposure to corporate operations, manufacturing processes, supply chain management, marketing strategies, and organisational behaviour in action.

As part of the experiential learning approach, students interact with industry experts, observe management practices, and gain insights into operational challenges and strategic decision-making.

  • Real-World Practice Exposure
  • Manufacturing Insight
  • Direct Expert Interaction
  • Program Core Component
💼

Summer Internship Programme (SIP)

The Summer Internship Programme (SIP) is an integral part of the MBA curriculum, designed to provide practical exposure through on-the-job training and live project work. The programme enables students to gain hands-on experience in areas such as Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations, and Systems.

Through SIP, students develop a deeper understanding of customer psychology, market dynamics, corporate culture, and real-time business challenges. Many internships also serve as a gateway to Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs) from reputed organizations.

Key Internship Domains:
🎯 Market Research 📈 Business Development 👥 HR & Talent Acquisition 💰 Financial Analysis 🚚 Operations & Supply Chain 💻 Systems & IT Analytics
